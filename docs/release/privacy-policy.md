# Privacy Policy (stub)

BusinessSajilo stores business data (products, customers, bills, orders) in Supabase hosted in your configured region. Authentication uses email/phone number and password. Push notification tokens may be stored for order and stock alerts.

## Account deletion

- Any customer or staff member can delete their own account from the app
  (customer app: account menu → "Delete my account"). Personal identity
  (name, phone, login) is permanently removed. Financial records (bills,
  payments, ledger entries) are the business's own records and are retained
  with the identity anonymized.
- A business owner can delete the entire business from Settings →
  "Delete business". This permanently removes all business data — products,
  bills, orders, customers, chat messages, uploaded images, and every staff
  and customer login. This action cannot be undone.
