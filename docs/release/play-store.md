## App name
BusinessSajilo

## Short description (80 chars)
Inventory, billing & udharo tracking for Nepali dealers and retailers.

## Full description
BusinessSajilo helps Nepali dealers and retailers manage inventory, create bills, track customer dues (udharo), and handle orders — in English and Nepali.

Features:
- Role-based apps for owner, sales, warehouse, and customers
- BS + AD dates on bills and ledger
- Offline billing for field staff with sync
- Low-stock alerts and reports
